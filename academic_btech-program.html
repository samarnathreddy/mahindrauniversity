B.Tech Computer Science & Engineering

Mahindra University École Centrale School of Engineering (MEC) will provide apprenticeship with collaboration from the industry, focus on humanities, international exposure and allied education to develop a ‘whole-brain-approach’ to business.

Core program

The B.Tech curriculum

4-Year B.Tech Program

The B.Tech curriculum is based on a core program in Basic Sciences (47 credits), Basic Engineering (28 credits), Humanities, Social and Creative Sciences (15 credits), Enterprise Sciences (10 credits).

The program aims at providing the students with:

  • A basic knowledge of the scientific-technological-social-business environment they are going to work in as practitioners. 
  • Methods and skills for analytical, critical, design thinking, experimental approaches, project management and communication.

Academic Regulations

Branch change

As per the Academic Regulations of Mahindra École Centrale, a change in the branch allotted to a student at the time of Admission is not possible in the second year. As such, a student admitted in a particular branch will eventually get his B. Tech. degree in that branch only.

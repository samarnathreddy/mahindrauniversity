New Engineers of the future.

Global standards of excellence

MEC is considered to be among the top engineering colleges in Hyderabad and even in India, as it grooms the New Engineers of the future. MEC follows a paradigm that transcends the conventional walls between academia-industry and theory-practice to usher in fresh ideas and deeper understanding

MEC rubs shoulders with global standards of excellence, housing laboratories that are equipped to effectuate ground-breaking inventions and innovations under the supervision of the faculty who are highly accomplished and dedicated scholars in their respective fields with industry, research and academic experience

MEC offers undergraduate courses in seven disciplines: B.Tech in Artificial Intelligence (AI)*, Civil Engineering (CE), Computation & Mathematics (CM)*, Computer Science and Engineering (CSE), Electronics & Computer Engineering (ECE)*, Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE) and Mechanical Engineering (ME)

*Subject to receiving government approval

The B.Tech curriculum is based on a core programme in Basic Sciences (47 credits), Basic Engineering (28 credits), Humanities, Social and Creative Sciences (15 credits), Enterprise Sciences (10 credits)

The curriculum aims at providing the students with:

  • A basic knowledge of the scientific-technological-social-business environment they are going to work in as practitioners
  • Methods and skills for developing analytical and critical thinking, experimental approaches, project management and communication

Rise of the New Engineer

With Mahindra Group and Centralesupélec

With the core intent of establishing one of the best engineering colleges in India and beyond, Mahindra University École Centrale School of Engineering (MEC) was born out of a collaboration between two giants: Mahindra Group - a leader of the industrial sector in India, and École Central Paris - a 180 year-old institute of eminence, instrumental in the progress of technology.

While Mahindra Group is a multinational organisation worth USD 17.8 billion, and 2,00,000 employees across 100 countries, whose businesses are driven primarily by engineering and technology; École Central Paris has been the alma mater to prestigious alumni whose contributions to the world have been pivotal: Gustave Eiffel, and founders of many renowned international organisations like Renault, Peugeot, Dior, Michelin, etc.

Having evolved from such illustrious parentage, MEC aims to reach the pinnacle of excellence in engineering education by offering industry-aligned undergraduate B.Tech courses that are globally relevant.

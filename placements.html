Placement Summary 2018

MEC students from the 2014 batch were interviewed by over 30 international brands and a majority of students opting for careers found placements among these recruiting companies.

These brands included corporate giants from Japan and global leaders like Microsoft, Mahindra & Mahindra Ltd., GAP Inc., Voltas among others. The average CTC was calculated to be 7.5 LPA and the highest CTC offered to some students was INR 30 LPA.

Placement Summary 2019

The 2nd batch 2015 -19 has begun the campus recruitment with an excellent start, attracting a wide range of recruiters - both Indian and International, across various industry verticals.

More than 40 recruiters have conducted campus, off-campus and internship processes, and selected almost 100% of the interested and eligible students. Some of the reputed recruiters are: Line Inc., Oisix Systems, Tecnos, Cisco, Capgemini, ZS Associates, Mahindra & Mahindra, TechnipFMC, K Raheja Corp, Intel, Mu Sigma, Jocata, ADP, PhenomPeople, Cognizant, Kasura, Cyient, Byjus, Tech Mahindra, Satyam-Venture, etc.

The average CTC has been in the range of Rs.7.5 LPA and the highest being Rs.45 LPA.
